Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026  ·  Effective for all SkyWay KOP users

SkyWay collects information to provide you the best flight booking experience. This includes personal identification information (name, email, phone, date of birth), passport/ID details for international bookings, payment information (processed securely and never stored in full), and booking preferences and travel history.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process your flight bookings and issue e-tickets
  • Send booking confirmations and boarding passes via email/WhatsApp
  • Provide customer support and resolve booking issues
  • Send relevant fare alerts and seasonal offers (opt-out available)
  • Improve our platform based on usage analytics
  • Comply with DGCA and government aviation regulations

3. Data Security

All data is protected using 256-bit SSL encryption. Payment transactions comply with PCI DSS standards. We never sell your personal data to third parties. Data is retained only as long as required by law or for service delivery.

4. Cookies

We use session cookies to maintain your login state and booking progress. Analytics cookies help us understand how passengers use our platform. You can disable cookies in your browser, though some features may not function correctly.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and download your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

6. Contact
